three named themes · drop into data-theme

cream warm · daylight

a quiet morning, somewhere.

this is what the body type looks like in cream. headings sit in lapis, body in deep plum on warm paper-white. accents come in waves: lavender, aqua, peach, lilac.

dusk indigo · evening

after the sun, before the stars.

same vocabulary, lower light. accents recede into the dark; muted text is the rule, not the exception.

paper monochrome · ink

just words, on a page.

no color. ink on paper. the muted line is a soft gray. for moments where the work should carry the room.
