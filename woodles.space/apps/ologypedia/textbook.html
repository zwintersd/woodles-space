    A textbook you grow

    This is one book, kept in your browser, that gets deeper the more you read. There's no blank page to face and no order you have to follow — you follow your own curiosity and the links catch up.

    1. Read an entry. Pick anything from the index on the left, or follow a link inside a page.
    2. Highlight to go deeper. Select any phrase — a name, a season, a term — and a small ✦ menu appears. Choose New entry and that phrase becomes its own page, already titled and already linked back to where you found it.
    3. Build it out whenever. A new page starts as a seed — that's a real, finished thing to be, not a chore. Come back and grow it when you want. Hit Edit to write, or just keep reading.
    4. Never lose your place. The trail at the top of each page is the path you took to get there; click any step to walk back. Everything saves itself as you go.
    Made to be low-friction on purpose. Starting a page never forces you to finish it, and never makes you leave the one you're on. Motion can be turned off (Calm motion), the room can be quieted down to one page (Focus), and the shape of every page is the same so nothing surprises you. Deep dives are the point, not a detour.
